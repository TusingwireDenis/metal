About ARAFAT METAL WORKS

Your trusted partner in metal fabrication and welding solutions

Serving Kisoro with Quality & Integrity Since 2021

Founded by Mr. Arafat, our company has grown from a small workshop to Kisoro's premier metal fabrication service provider. We take pride in our Ugandan roots and commitment to excellence in every project we undertake.

We offer competitive and negotiable pricing to ensure our high-quality metal work is accessible to all customers in Kisoro and surrounding areas. Our team will work with you to find solutions that fit your budget without compromising on quality.
